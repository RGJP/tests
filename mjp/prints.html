Books & Prints

Photo Books

Marius offers three photography books that each take you on a distinct, meditative journey of contemplation, while maintaining the cohesive thread of his unique artistic vision. The three volumes—Bruine, la pointe de la Gaspésie, Passages, and his latest bold project, Sonde—present immersive experiences through carefully curated photographic encounters. Each book is designed to bring the artist's perspective into your space. Accessible to all, they offer a natural and direct connection to the artwork.

Fine Art Prints

Bring the vibrant energy of nature into your space through the artist’s unique vision—captured in a premium fine art print, expertly crafted and shipped directly from the studio, complete with a hand-signed Certificate of Authenticity.
